It's not just Coffee
It's StarBucks

Starbucks is a well-known coffeehouse chain that was founded in Seattle, Washington in 1971. Today, it has over 31,000 locations worldwide, making it one of the largest coffee chains in the world. One of the reasons for Starbucks' success is its commitment to quality. The company sources high-quality coffee beans from around the world and roasts them in small batches to ensure freshness and flavor. Additionally, Starbucks offers a wide variety of coffee and tea drinks, as well as snacks and food items, so there is something for everyone.
